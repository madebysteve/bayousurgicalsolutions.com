Knotless anatomic fixation system for rotator cuff repair by KFx Medical

Bayou Surgical Solutions is the sole distributor for the KFx knotless rotator cuff repair system in the East Texas and North Louisiana area.

The SutureCross System

The SutureCross system is intended to be used on both mini-open and arthroscopic double row rotator cuff repair.

The product consists of a fixation device pre-loaded with non-absorbable #2 suture in an inserter handle. The handle consists of a 3mm medial cobalt alloy implant which deploys into 10mm of fixation and a 5.5 or 6.3 mm lateral titanium screw.

Features include:

  • double row, single row or pasta procedure repair options
  • can be used mini-open or arthroscopic
  • no instrumentation needed (except a mallet)
  • easy to use and fast to place!
  • transtendon option
  • minimized MRI scatter
  • complete antomic footprint restoration
  • superior compression of cuff
  • reduces gap formation by 50% vs. knotted systems
